About

A small tool for seeing a big picture.

iFootprint is a lightweight carbon footprint calculator built to make environmental impact easier to understand. It focuses on a few everyday inputs: home energy, waste, recycling, travel, and eating habits.

This refreshed version preserves the original calculator logic while giving the site a cleaner, simpler, more durable design.

Built as an Ibacus project.
